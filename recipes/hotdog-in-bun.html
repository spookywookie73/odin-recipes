A Hotdog in a Bun

Juicy Hotdogs in a Bun

Boiled tasty Hotdogs in a Slightly toasted bun with a dash of ketchup and mustard.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tin of Hotdogs
  • 1 pack of Hotdog buns (preferably sliced open).
  • Condiments

Recipe

  1. Open your tin of Hotdogs and pour the contents into a pan.
  2. Put the pan over a high heat.
  3. Put the buns under the grill until they are slightly toasted on top.
  4. Once the liquid in the pan is bubbling, take the pan off the heat and drain the liquid away.
  5. Place 2 hotdogs in each bun and cover with Ketchup and/or Mustard.
