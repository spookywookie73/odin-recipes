Scrambled egg on toast

Golden Scrambled egg on thick sliced Toast

Use large free-range eggs together with a thick slice of crusty bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • Butter
  • 1 Thick Slice of Crusty Bread

Recipe

  1. Break both eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt and pepper and mix together with a fork until golden.
  2. If not already sliced, cut a thick slice of bread.
  3. Toast your bread under the grill until it is a medium dark brown colour. Only toast one side.
  4. While the bread is toasting, melt some butter in a pan and then add the eggs. Once the eggs start to cook, use a spatula to pull the eggs from the edges of the pan towards the middle.
  5. If your bread is toasted enough, place it on a plate and spread butter over it. Put it to one side.
  6. Keep mixing the eggs together, breaking up any large portions of egg. Keep doing this for a few minutes until the eggs look soft but not runny.
  7. Pour the contents of the pan over your toast. Sprinkle a pinch of salt over the eggs and enjoy a delicious meal.
